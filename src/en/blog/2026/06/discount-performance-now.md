Members of Fronteers, we have a nice discount for you if you would like to attend Performance.now(), the web performance conference brought to you by the same team as CSS Day. On the 19th and 20th of November, expect Harry Roberts, Stéphanie Walter, Tammy Everts, Tim Kadlec and Pat Meenan to take the stage, with Robin Marx presenting as MC. The event takes place at the Zuiderkerk in Amsterdam.

Fronteers members get a 100 euro discount on tickets. But act quickly, because on Wednesday July 1st prices are expected to go up. Until then, tickets with our discount are 600 euro (VAT excluded). Please mail the board at [bestuur@fronteers.nl](mailto:bestuur@fronteers.nl) if you're interested!
