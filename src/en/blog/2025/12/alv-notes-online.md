The [minutes for our general membership meeting](/en/organisation/board/notes/notulen-alv-28-november-2025.html) have been added to the site! You can still also look at the [slides](/downloads/slides/Fronteers-ALV-2025-slides.pdf) (PDF, 4,6 MB).

During the meeting, the following points came up for the board to pay special attention to:

### Finance & Administration

1. **Investigate SBI code change at Chamber of Commerce**
   - Advice from the Audit Committee
   - Can be done for free online
   - Consider reviewing the statutes at the same time if a notary is needed

2. **Evaluate trademark registration**
   - Currently registered at a board member's address
   - Change for UK costs ~€1000, EU registration also ~€1000
   - Decision needed on whether this is worth it

### Board & Organization

3. **Recruit new board members**
   - General board position (one board member stepping down)
   - Treasurer position (current treasurer stepping down after 3 years)
   - Current treasurer will do handover for new treasurer

4. **Create job descriptions**
   - Description for board positions (general board member)
   - Description for treasurer position
   - List of what Fronteers needs from board members and volunteers

### Marketing & Communication

5. **Create marketing plan for 2026**
   - Improve marketing of what Fronteers does and events
   - Discuss in next board meeting
   - Marketing committee can pick up actions from new marketing plan

6. **Website improvements**
   - Place more content on website and publish things earlier
   - Add interviews with developers
   - Set up content calendar and stick to it
   - Improve events calendar to clearly show all upcoming events

7. **Improve accessibility information**
   - Better communication about accessibility of event locations
   - Include information on website as well
   - Share proactively instead of reactively

### Events & Activities

8. **Develop "Ask the expert" sessions**
   - Further develop the concept
   - Consider interview-style as an alternative

9. **Dark Mode Conference 2026**
   - Start communication earlier
   - If ticket sales go well, immediately start planning the 2027 version

10. **Plan workshops**
    - Plan 2 accessibility workshops for 2026
    - Add to budget

11. **Bring back book club**
    - Plan for 2026

### Volunteers

12. **Improve volunteer management**
    - Brainstorm about how to assign tasks to volunteers
    - More active involvement of volunteers
    - Create list of volunteer tasks
    - Better communication with volunteers (sometimes it works better to assign a task to someone specific)

13. **Support Activities Committee**
    - Volunteers needed to host/organize meetups
    - Represent Fronteers during meetups

### Other

14. **Handle leftover items**
    - Give away board games and other items to members
    - Currently stored with some board members

15. **Check phone number status**
    - Is it used for conferences?
    - Is it used for Chamber of Commerce?
    - Determine status for 2026
