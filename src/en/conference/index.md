## About Fronteers Dark Mode

Fronteers Dark Mode is a cozy, single-track afterwork front-end conference by and for the community. Taking place on Friday, October 2nd, 2026 at the atmospheric Cinema De Witt in Dordrecht, it offers an evening of inspiring talks on topics such as accessibility, sustainability, HTML, CSS – with of course a nerdy late-night finale.

## Tickets

Tickets will be available soon for the fixed price of just **€99**. Tickets include entry, a delicious 3-course dinner and drink vouchers.

**Fronteers members pay only €49!** You can still [become a member](/en/become-a-member/) ;-)

Ticket sales will start in January 2026 and will be announced via our newsletter and social media. [Subscribe to our newsletter](/en/information/newsletter/) to stay updated. *Ticket prices are including VAT.*

## Location

Filmtheater De Witt - an atmospheric cinema, brasserie and cultural stage in the historic center of Dordrecht.

The cinema and brasserie are wheelchair accessible. There are no steps or other obstacles in the building. There is also a lift in the building. There is also an accessible toilet available on the ground floor.

## Code of Conduct

Fronteers Conference does not tolerate harassment of any sort at any of our locations. Harassment includes offensive comments related to gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, deliberate intimidation, stalking, (physical) following, harassing photography or recording, sustained disruption of talks or other events, inappropriate physical contact, and unwelcome (sexual) attention.

Please contact the Fronteers staff if you feel you are being harassed, if you see someone being harassed, or if you have any related concerns:

* Find a crew member IRL (identified by a branded crew t-shirt)
* Email us at [conference@fronteers.nl](mailto:conference@fronteers.nl)

The Fronteers community has proven year after year to be a very welcoming and open group. By joining that group, you will be held to that same high standard.

The Fronteers: Dark Mode organisers reserve the right to enforce adherence to the Code of Conduct, by measures up to and including expulsion from our events.
