The Digital Summit brings people together around a central question: How do we give direction to digitalisation in a democratic, sustainable and just society?

Conference for members and non-members. This edition explores how we can maintain or regain societal grip on our education system, how public organisations across Europe can become more digitally autonomous, and how policymakers can contribute to sustainable digitalisation. The summit is chaired by Piek Knijff and the keynote will be given by Felienne Hermans, professor of computer science education at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.

- **Date:** Saturday 11 April 2026, 12:30 – 18:30
- **Location:** Tolhuistuin, Amsterdam
