FrontKon returns for its fifth edition in Prague, bringing together frontend developers, designers, and web professionals for a day of deep technical talks, community connections, and inspiration. The conference features three stages, international speakers, and a programme that goes far beyond the basics of frontend development.

Organized by the [Frontendisti.cz community](https://www.frontendisti.cz/), FrontKon combines high-end content with a welcoming community atmosphere and plenty of opportunities to meet the people shaping the future of frontend development. In 2026, the event becomes even more international with an English-friendly format and speakers from across Europe and beyond.

- **Date:** Monday 5th & Tuesday 6th of October 2026
- **Location:** O2 Universum, Prague, Czech Republic
- **Expected attendance:** 900 attendees
- **Format:** Three stages with talks in Czech and English
- **Website:** [frontkon.tech](https://www.frontkon.tech)
