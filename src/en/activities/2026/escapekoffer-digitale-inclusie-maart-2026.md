During the Week of Health Inequalities, you can play the Escapekoffer Digitale Inclusie on Monday 16 March at Digitaal Toegankelijk on Plompetorengracht 4 in Utrecht.

After playing the game, you'll understand what it's like when digital care is made too difficult for you. Curious about last year's edition? Watch the video!

- **Date:** Monday 16 March 2026
- **Location:** Digitaal Toegankelijk, Plompetorengracht 4, Utrecht
- **Sign up:** [Via this link](https://digitaaltoegankelijk.nl/escape-koffer-maandag/)

On the [Week of Health Inequalities](https://www.pharos.nl/weekvandegezondheidsverschillen/) website you can find more events throughout the Netherlands.
